About

I am interested in building mathematical and computational models to connect extracellular signaling, transcription factor activity, and downstream gene expression. My recent work focuses on integrating cell–cell communication, gene regulatory networks, perturbation modeling, and spatial reconstruction in single-cell systems.

Research Interests

Cell–Cell Communication and GRN Coupling

Linking ligand–receptor signaling to TF activation and target gene responses with interpretable models.

Single-cell and Spatial Omics

Analyzing spatial transcriptomics, pseudobulk trends, and cell-state transitions in biological systems.

Mathematical Modeling

Using dynamical systems, diffusion-inspired models, and probabilistic frameworks for biological regulation.

Computational Methods

Developing robust machine learning pipelines for perturbation prediction, network refinement, and representation learning.
